COD Rolloff Pricing – Residential Homeowners -


These COD rolloffs are priced via AMP.  Please be sure that:
Contract Term - 0 months
Temporary – must be checked
Container Usage Charge – $35 after 7 days
Est. Tons Per Haul – 4 tons or exempt if paperwork calls it out
Est. Hauls per month – No less than 1
Haul rate is Actual Haul Charge per Haul (includes up to 4 tons)
Delivery is $150.00


COD Payment

Use EZpay account 000000000020076 to process payment.  Type payment information as follows:

Type payment, amount of payment, confirmation #, Service Name, Service Address

Example: Visa $1254.32/Cheryl Smith/2555 Meridian Blvd Ste 200 Franklin, TN


Credit/Debit Card are preferred payments.  If customer pays via ACH, you must wait 10 business days before processing delivery due to issues with declined payments.  Verify payment has cleared by searching payment via WM EZPay after 10 business days.
